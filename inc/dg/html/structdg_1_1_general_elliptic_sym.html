Detailed Description

template<class Geometry, class Matrix, class Vector>
struct dg::GeneralEllipticSym< Geometry, Matrix, Vector >

Operator that acts as a 3d negative elliptic differential operator. Is the symmetric of the GeneralElliptic with 0.5(D_+ + D_-) or vice versa.

The term discretized is

\[ -\nabla \cdot ( \mathbf b \mathbf b \cdot \nabla ) \]

In general that means

\[ \begin{align} v = b^x \partial_x f + b^y\partial_y f + b^z \partial_z f \\ -\frac{1}{\sqrt{g}} \left(\partial_x(\sqrt{g} b^x v ) + \partial_y(\sqrt{g}b_y v) + \partial_z(\sqrt{g} b_z v)\right) \end{align} \]

is discretized, with \( b^i\) being the contravariant components of \(\mathbf b\) .
