Detailed Description

1D grid for X-point topology

The grid looks like |---—x-------—x---—| , where the x indicates the periodicity and - the number of points The left boundary is x0 and the right x1, the inner boundaries lie at x0 + f*Lx and x1-f*Lx therefore f must be smaller than 0.5
